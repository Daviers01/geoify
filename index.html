How it works

  1. Upload any image (JPEG, PNG, WebP, etc.).
  2. Pick a location by clicking the map, using your device's GPS, or typing decimal coordinates manually.
  3. Click Geotag & Download — the GPS data is embedded directly into the image's EXIF metadata.
  4. Download and open in any EXIF-aware app (Maps, Photos, online viewers) to see the location pin.

Privacy: All processing happens entirely in your browser. No image data is ever uploaded to a server.
